Hi!

I'm Jacopo Ammendola. Architect, designer, scholar, writer.
I live in Firenze, Italy. This is my personal website.

Some years ago I cofounded orizzontale, a collective/practice/lab that crosses design disciplines revolving around the theme of public space.

Since June 2020 I'm one of the editors of vuoto, a transmedial public space that hosts conversations about unstableness, urbanism, architecture and design.

I also currently work at the Department of Architecture of the University of Florence as a teacher, researcher and designer of exhibitions, books, wayfinding systems, websites and mobile apps.
